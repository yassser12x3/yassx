Introduction to Genshin Impact

Genshin Impact is an open-world action role-playing game developed and published by miHoYo. It was released in September 2020 and has become one of the most popular games in the world. The game features a vast, beautiful world to explore, a variety of characters to collect and develop, and engaging gameplay mechanics.

Top Tips for Beginners

If you're new to Genshin Impact, here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Explore the world and complete quests to earn rewards.
  • Collect and upgrade characters to build a strong team.
  • Participate in daily and weekly challenges for additional rewards.
